About Us

Have you ever had a business idea you though were great until you told someone about it, then you realised I really have not thought this through?

Registered interest gives you the opportunity to put an idea out there and receive feedback allowing you to develop and evolve the concept through a likeminded community of business-oriented people.

Feedback is designed to give you the opportunity to put the ideas out there to survey the community upvote or downvote your favourite ideas and make friends in an online community.


Registered Interest is a website to air out your business idea and inspire you to develop the idea further and think more deeply about how to deliver it or make it unique and successful.

It is the sense test ideas platform where you can thrash out your business idea, receive feedback, upvotes and downvotes and even find your next business partner or investment.
